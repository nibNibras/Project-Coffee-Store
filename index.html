About Us

why choose us ?

At Aroma di Colore, we believe coffee is more than a drink — it’s a feeling. Our café was created as a space to pause, to breathe, and to savor moments that matter. Inspired by the charm of Italian streets and the softness of watercolor art, every detail is crafted with care — from the warmth of our espresso to the colors that surround you.

Our Menu

Crema Classica

A timeless Italian espresso — rich, bold, and
kissed with a velvety crema. Perfectly balanced
and full of soul, it’s the heart of the café in a single sip.

Moka Pennellata

Dark chocolate swirls meet deep espresso in
this mocha masterpiece. Creamy, comforting,
and artfully topped with a soft milk foam — in every cup.

Cappuccino Sereno

A peaceful harmony of espresso and frothy milk,
finished with a dusting of cocoa. Light,and gentle —
the kind of drink that turns mornings into quiet rituals.

Matcha al Latte Verde

A gentle green tea latte, earthy and
smooth with a touch of sweetness. Vibrant in color
and calming in spirit — like sipping a painted meadow.

Panino Rustico

A warm ciabatta sandwich filled with fresh
mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and a drizzle of homemade
pesto. Simple, rustic, and full of Italian countryside charm.

Pizza Fiorita

A petite artisan pizza with mozzarella, cherry tomatoes,
and basil, arranged like a blooming canvas. Fresh, colorful,
and full of charm — a slice of Italy, painted with flavor.
